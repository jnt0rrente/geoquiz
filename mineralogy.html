Mineralogy

Definition

Mineralogy is a discipline of geology. It specializes in the study of the physical, chemical and structural properties of minerals. It is of utmost importance for many branches of modern engineering and technology, such as materials engineering and circuit manufacture.

Properties of a mineral

Among the different properties a given mineral can present for its study, some of the most relevant are its color, hardness, density and tenacity. Below are listed some of these properties for some of the most well-known minerals.
